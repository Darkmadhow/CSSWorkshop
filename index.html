Not another Hello World

Seriously, the amount of Hello World projects in this world is way too high. So we decided against it - the rubber duck agreed. So, you are here to have some tips for web development and where to get help; look no further!

Have you heard of Rubber Duck?

No, not the truck driver from the movie "Convoy". The actual rubber duck you deny taking into the bath tub with you. If you ever see a programmer talk to one of those, don't assume he's crazy, he is just bouncing off his ideas to reflect on them. Try it yourself - if you can explain the concept of your site or code to a rubber duck, it makes sense. If you can't explain it to a rubber duck, you should definitely rethink your project.
